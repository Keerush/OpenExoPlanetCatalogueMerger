Our Team Agreements:

  1. We will communicate mainly using Slack. We will also message through Skype, Facebook, texts or emails if necessary.

  2. Response times should at be within a day. Team members should check Slack at least once a day.

  3. We will meet at least once a week on Fridays after CSCC01 tutorial.

  4. Expect to meet at school in the IC labs, BV labs, or in any available study space. Also expect any online message meetings if necessary.

  5. Don't push code that does not compile, or breaks any main features. Commits should have a meaningful message about what has changed. Drastic changes to code should be consulted with other team members.

  6. All work should be divided equally among members or based on each member's strengths after planning/discussion. Members unable to finish work should bring it up with team members.

  7. All members should do review before submitting. Expect submission to be within a day of deadline. Submitter will be picked during day of the submission.

  8. Any major conflicts will be brought up with Anya or our TA.
